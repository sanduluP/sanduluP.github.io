Who Am I?

I'm Priyabanta Sandulu and a M.Sc Data Science student at Universitat Trier, Germany. I work and talk about Data Analysis, Visualizations, Case-based reasoning, and Statistical analysis.

As a Research Assistant at DFKI German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence, I focus on RPA process creation and analysis using BPMN. I also excel in semantic knowledge modeling and implementing AI services like Case-based reasoning(CBR) and semantic search.

In my previous roles as a Test Data Consultant at Tata Consultancy Services and AVIVA Insurance, I excelled in global teamwork, ensuring GDPR compliance through data handling techniques. As a Test Automation Analyst, I proficiently developed and managed automation frameworks for web and API testing, using Selenium, QTP, Jenkins, GitHub, Cucumber, SQL, Python, and Java.
